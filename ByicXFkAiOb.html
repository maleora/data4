More About In a New York Minute

It was a nice quick read to kick off the year. I get that it was a short story but it was TOO short to actually have any substance to it. Yes, it got me into a wintery mood and it was nice to sit and read it while sipping hot chocolate by the fire but I was done in about an hour or less. I would've liked a bit more plot development... This was probably a 1.5 for me. I'm sure another reader might love it. I found it choppy and disjointed and the tenses were very strange. The heroine was unsympathetic and for me personally it didn't work. I couldn't even go on to read the start of the book that followed the short story as for me this was one that just wasn't my cup... Not even worth a star :(

More About Christmas in Paris

As a novel, this book fails spectacularly. The heroine, three days before what must be an extremely expensive Christmas wedding, has decided to call it off because he has suddenly decided to eschew his corporate life and become a farmer. OK, I can understand that she would break off the engagement. However, there are no tears, no calling... This is what I get for picking a book based on a pretty cover. (And a pretty concept.) I was looking for something sweet and Christmasy to put me in the Christmas mood. But this story was ridiculous. The characters were bizarre and very thinly drawn, and nothing they did actually made sense. I will say the ending was better than I expected... Not completely realistic, but who wants realistic at Christmas IN Paris? This novel had all the elements of a love story that made it delightful to read. Besides, who doesn't love Paris and hearing about all of the amazing locations and sights there?

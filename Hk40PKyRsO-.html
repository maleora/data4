More About The Teahouse of the August Moon

Much better and funnier than the movie. The Teahouse of the August Moon falls into the category of a service comedy. There is no active war but a majority of the characters are in the military. It is in the same vein as What did you do in the war Daddy? and Rally round the Flag boys. I liked how the story dealt with people doing what they wanted rather than what was required....

More About A Girl Like Sugar

A Girl Like Sugar was an interesting look at a young woman growing out of the shadow of her more exceptional friends and family, mainly her dead rock star boyfriend and her activist mother. Over all the book was quite well written, creating a vivid portrait of Toronto in the early 2000s even with many of the locations' names' changed.... I was attracted to this book because it's based in Toronto ... as it turns out, Sugar doesn't live far from me.Definitely not a boring cast of characters! And I liked the story and was cheering for Sugar.I enjoyed it because it was wacky and different and dark. I think I would have liked this book a lot more if I read it when I was a teenager. That said, I'm not sure what teenage me would have thought of the incredibly graphic sex scenes?I wanted more ghost action. Served as a neat opportunity to approach mental health but didn't take it all the way.

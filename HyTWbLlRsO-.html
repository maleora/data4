More About All-Star Western, Volume 4: Gold Standard

This kicks off a three-volume arc that was better than I expected. Normally I read Jonah Hex to enjoy the no-brain-required shoot 'em up style, but here Hex has his world turned upside down. Amadeus Arkham drops out of the story, but that's ok because the new scenario is pretty fun. Another solid entry by Palmiotti and Gray in what has become one of the longest Western series in recent American comic books (I'm not forgetting Italy's Tex or longer running series from the 60s).

More About Captain Newman, M. D

I learned that it is the weak who are cruel, and that gentleness is to be expected only from the strong. Leo Rosten, Captain Newman, M. D //

Read this book when I was a young or pre-teen. Very funny/serious novel later made into a very good movie. Interesting story of a psychologist who is a doctor in a hospital near the end of WWII. Most of the stories were interesting. This classic is a wonderfully funny, moving read. Has stuck in my head since I read it in the early 1980s and have re-read several times. Nice picture of the writing style of this era, as well. Like watching a Doris Day flick.

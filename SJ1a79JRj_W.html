Interesting to relearn how WWII transformed the US into what it is today; kind of made me want to plant a Victory Garden. Or do Victory Knitting. Go pet my Victory Bunny. I read several of these World War II Time-Life books during our WWII school year. These were some of the best and most interesting books.

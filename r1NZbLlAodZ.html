More About G.I. Combat, Vol. 1: The War That Time Forgot

Three separate worlds, all a tribute and credit to the classic gonzo war comics of the past. A minor quibble,this title was cancelled after only seven issues, so it would more appropriately be titled "The Complete" rather than "Volume 1". The Unknown Soldier was the best part. Small stories about minor characters. These are just creators having fun with little to no stakes. I have no real association with any of these characters so I can't say whether or not they are respectful of what came before but I enjoyed the stories.

More About The Heart of It

The invitation on her face was as old as Eve. As potent as original sin. Molly O'Keefe, The Heart Of It //

4.5 StarsM. O'Keefe is not an author. She's a magician. The amount of emotion and depth she managed to infuse into 67 pages is down right magical. I've read books three times the length of this novella that were lacking in character development. I only knew Gabe and Elena for a handful of pages but I felt like I'd lived with them for... Tackling an emotional subject such as child abuse is difficult for any author but to be able to wrap the subject in and around a love story is a great talent indeed. Molly OKeefe has a special touch with this story by choosing to address child abuse victims as they learn to care for another, learn how to enjoy pleasure, and how to love... Went in completely blind and loved it so much. Thanks, Anna for introducing me to the book!
